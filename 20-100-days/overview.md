# 100 days project — overview

## Why this works for your specific goals

A 100 day project gives you a story with a beginning, middle, and end — the most powerful content format that exists. People don't just follow posts. They follow journeys.

| Benefit | What it means for you |
|---|---|
| Authority | 100 builds = undeniable proof. You didn't just study AI — you shipped 100 things with it. |
| Consistency | You never wonder what to post. Every day is a build, every build is a post. |
| Discovery | #100DaysOfBuilding already has an audience. You're joining a stream, not starting from silence. |
| Research | By day 30 you'll know which builds get 10x more engagement — those become your courses and tools. |

---

## What it is

A public commitment to build 100 small, useful things — documenting every single one across your website, LinkedIn, X, Instagram, and YouTube.

**Start date:** June 10, 2026
**End date:** September 17, 2026
**Hashtag:** #100DaysOfBuilding

---

## What counts as one "build"

- A working script or tool (even 50 lines of Python)
- A working prototype — rough but functional
- A new feature or automation
- A Cursor prompt template or workflow
- A dashboard or visualisation
- A website feature or component
- An educational app or interactive learning tool

**Rule:** It must be real and runnable. Not a plan. Something you can show working.

## Days 1–10 — AI builds (establish builder identity)

- **Day 1:** AI caption generator for artists — Claude API. Takes artwork description → generates 5 Instagram caption options

- **Days 4–5:** Document Figma → UI build using Cursor at Ainsemble (share what's appropriate)
- **Days 6–7:** Cursor workflow template — free resource for dev audience. Reusable prompts and rules for common tasks
- **Days 8–10:** Build log generator — paste rough notes about a build, get a formatted LinkedIn post draft

## Days 11–30 — Knowledge sharing builds (educational tools)

- **Days 11–13:** Kids vocabulary app — AI-powered word of the day with image and sentence. Simple, shareable
- **Days 14–16:** Flashcard generator — paste any topic, get study cards. Useful for students and self-learners
- **Days 17–19:** Explain It Simply tool — paste any complex concept, get a plain-English explanation at 3 levels (kid / teen / adult)
- **Days 20–22:** Daily habit tracker with AI reflection — log your day, get a one-line insight back
- **Days 23–25:** Story starter for kids — AI generates a story opening, child continues it. Creative writing prompt engine
- **Days 26–28:** Quiz generator — paste a topic or paste notes, get a 10-question quiz. Works for any subject
- **Days 29–30:** Document the knowledge sharing theme — what resonated, what got shared, what to double down on
