## Meta ads plan

### Phase 1 — Warm audience first (free, do this week)

**Step 1:** Install Meta Pixel on debasreedeyart.com (if not already)
- This builds a custom audience of everyone who visits the site
- These people already know the brand — warmest possible ad audience

**Step 2:** Create a lookalike audience
- Once you have 100+ pixel visitors, Meta finds people who look like them
- Artists, creatives, people interested in art courses — globally or India-focused

**Step 3:** Post organically first on Instagram + Facebook
- Before spending money, post the debasreedeyart.com build story organically
- See what engagement it gets
- Best organic post becomes your first ad creative

### Phase 2 — First paid campaign ($5–$10/day)

| Setting | Value |
|---|---|
| Ad objective | Lead generation form — not website traffic |
| Budget | $5–$10/day ($150–$300/month) |
| Creative | "I built this for my wife's art business. Now helping other artists." + screenshot of debasreedeyart.com |
| Targeting | Artists, art teachers, craft sellers, Etsy sellers, painting interest |
| Lookalike | From debasreedeyart.com pixel audience |

**Why lead gen form not website traffic:**
Artist fills in name, email, what they need → you get a qualified lead directly in your inbox. No landing page needed to start.

### Phase 3 — Scale what works

- Run two ad creatives simultaneously
- Kill the underperforming one after 7 days
- Scale the winner slowly — add $2–$5/day per week
- One client at $1,000 = 6x return on $150/month ad spend

### Important note
Talk to Debasree before using her site in ads. She almost certainly will be comfortable but worth the conversation first.
