## Meta ads plan

### Phase 1 — Free (this week)
1. Install Meta Pixel on debasreedeyart.com if not already there
2. Build custom audience from site visitors (wait for 100+ visitors)
3. Post organically first — best organic post becomes first ad creative

### Phase 2 — First paid campaign
- **Budget:** $5–$10/day ($150–$300/month)
- **Objective:** Lead generation form — not website traffic
- **Creative:** Before/after or "here's what I built" — screenshot of debasreedeyart.com
- **Targeting:** Artists, art teachers, craft sellers, Etsy sellers, art class interest
- **Lookalike:** From debasreedeyart.com pixel audience

### Phase 3 — Scale
- Kill underperforming ad after 7 days
- Scale winner slowly — add $2–$5/day per week
- One client at $1,000 = 6x return on $150 ad spend

### Important
Talk to Debasree before using her site in ads — she almost certainly will be comfortable but worth the conversation.
