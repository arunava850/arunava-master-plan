# Flywheel + opportunity map

## The core flywheel

Everything you do feeds everything else. Nothing goes to waste.

```
BUILD something real
    ↓
DOCUMENT it honestly
    ↓
POST it on 3 platforms
    ↓
AUDIENCE grows + trusts you
    ↓
People hire you or buy your course
    ↓
Revenue funds more building
    ↓
(repeat — compounds over time)
```

**Every single thing you build has three lives:**
1. A demo — proof you can do it
2. A piece of content — builds your audience
3. Authority proof — replaces the need for a certificate

## The three paths to independence

### Path 1 — Quick income (consulting + freelance)
- **What:** AI consulting + artist website builds
- **Timeline:** Income in 4–8 weeks
- **Your edge:** Ship fast with Cursor + AI. Rare in freelance market.
- **Cricket angle:** Cricket org AI consulting — boards, academies, media
- **Ceiling:** $10k–$30k/mo (time-bound — trades time for money)

### Path 2 — Build to sell (micro-SaaS / AI tools)
- **What:** Solve one problem, sell access
- **Timeline:** First $ in 3–6 months
- **Your edge:** Build in days not months. Ainsemble gives you real patterns.
- **Cricket angle:** Cricket analytics SaaS — coaches, scouts, fantasy
- **Ceiling:** Acqui-hire or passive ARR

### Path 3 — Build to own (personal brand + audience)
- **What:** Content + audience + courses + products
- **Timeline:** Compounds over 12–24 months
- **Your edge:** Build in public with real ships. Show, don't tell.
- **Cricket angle:** AI + cricket content brand — huge passionate audience
- **Ceiling:** Unlimited — you own the asset

### Recommended combo
**Path 1 funds you → Path 2 and 3 build leverage**

Start with artist website builds (Path 1 income this week) while the 100 days project builds Path 3 audience — which eventually sells Path 2 products.

```

```

## What makes this flywheel different from most people's

Most people separate "building" from "marketing" from "selling." They're three different activities that compete for time.

For you — they're the same activity.

- Building the cricket analytics model IS your marketing (people watch you do it)
- Documenting the artist website build IS your sales pitch (clients see the proof)
- Writing the LinkedIn post IS your course curriculum (becomes the lesson later)

**The 100 days project is the engine that makes all three happen simultaneously.**
