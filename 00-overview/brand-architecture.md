# Brand architecture + domain strategy

## The structure

```
Arthagu LLC  ← invisible legal backbone — all revenue flows through here
    │
    ├── debasreedeyart.com   ← Debasree's art courses (existing, keep as-is)
    ├── dearart.com          ← Artist website building service (PENDING DECISION)
    ├── arunavamondal.com    ← Personal brand + courses + 100 days project
    └── arthagu.com          ← Arthagu company/holding page (activate later)
```

---

## Why NOT to use arthagu.com for the artist service

Arthagu LLC is the umbrella company. In 2 years it will house AI tools, cricket analytics products, a course platform, consulting, and more. Locking arthagu.com into "we build artist websites" now undersells it and creates a brand mismatch later.

Keep arthagu.com free to become whatever makes sense as the business grows.

---

## Why dearart.com works for the artist service

- Warm, artist-facing, immediately understandable
- "Dear Art" speaks directly to the artist audience emotionally
- Perfectly scoped — doesn't try to be a tech company
- Already owned — no new domain cost
- "We build beautiful homes for your art online" lands perfectly under this brand

## Social profile strategy — two parallel identities

### Identity 1 — Arunava Mondal (personal brand)

| Platform | Handle | Content |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | Arunava Mondal | AI builds, cricket, build-in-public — primary authority platform |
| X / Twitter | @arunavamondal or @arunava_builds | Cricket hot takes, AI tips, build updates |
| Threads | @arunavamondal | Repurpose from X — growing fast in India |
| YouTube | Arunava Mondal | Build-along videos, cricket analytics walkthroughs |

### Identity 2 — Arthagu / Dear Art (service brand)

| Platform | Handle | Content |
|---|---|---|
| Instagram | @dearart or @arthagu | Artist website builds, before/after, visual content |
| Facebook Page | Dear Art / Arthagu | Artist service, Meta ads run from here |
| Threads | @dearart or @arthagu | Connects automatically from Instagram |

**Rule:** Never mix the two identities. LinkedIn stays personal brand only — no service promotion. Artist service stays on Instagram and Facebook only — protects job privacy.
