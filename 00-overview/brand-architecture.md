## Why NOT to use arthagu.com for the artist service

Arthagu LLC is the umbrella company. In 2 years it will house AI tools, cricket analytics products, a course platform, consulting, and more. Locking arthagu.com into "we build artist websites" now undersells it and creates a brand mismatch later.

Keep arthagu.com free to become whatever makes sense as the business grows.

---

## Why dearart.com works for the artist service

- Warm, artist-facing, immediately understandable
- "Dear Art" speaks directly to the artist audience emotionally
- Perfectly scoped — doesn't try to be a tech company
- Already owned — no new domain cost
- "We build beautiful homes for your art online" lands perfectly under this brand
- **Confirmed by Debasree** (April 22, 2026) ✅
