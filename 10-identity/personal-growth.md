# Personal Growth

> A living book for myself. Not for publishing, not for revenue. This is about who I am, who I want to become, and what I want to leave behind. Update freely — no format rules, no deadlines. Just honesty.

---

## How to use this

- Write in any section when something becomes clear
- Answer the prompts when you're ready — skip them when you're not
- Add dated entries as things evolve — this is a living document, not a one-time exercise
- Plans & Tasks at the bottom are for actual actions you commit to

## Five-Year Future — A Normal Tuesday

> It is five years from today. Describe a single ordinary day in your life. Not your best day. Not a dream vacation. Not the day you win an award. Just a normal Tuesday. Write in the present tense, as though you are already there. **Important: Do not write what you think is realistic. Write what feels deeply right.**

### Morning

- Where do you wake up?
- What does your bedroom look like? What is outside your window?
- What time do you get up? How do you feel when you wake up?
- Are you excited about the day ahead?

## Knowledge Building
