# Certifications path

## Philosophy

Certifications run in the background alongside building — not as a prerequisite to starting. You are already qualified by your output. Certs deepen knowledge and open specific doors.

**Start with DeepLearning.AI this week.** It's free to audit and takes a few weeks. "I'm doing Andrew Ng's LLM course alongside building cricket analytics" is already a LinkedIn post.

---

## Phase 1 — Do now (free or low cost)

### DeepLearning.AI — AI for Everyone
- **Why:** Foundational AI literacy. Widely recognised. Shows you understand AI beyond just tools.
- **Time:** 2–3 weeks
- **Cost:** Free to audit · $49 for certificate
- **Status:** `[ ]` Not started

### DeepLearning.AI — LLM specialisation
- **Why:** Directly applicable to what you're building. Agents, prompting, RAG, fine-tuning.
- **Time:** 4–6 weeks
- **Cost:** ~$49/month Coursera
- **Status:** `[ ]` Not started

## Phase 2 — Next (3–6 months)
